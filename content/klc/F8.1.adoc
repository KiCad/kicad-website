. **Documentation** field contains comma-separated device information. Where appropriate, a URL to the footprint datasheet should be included.
. **Keywords** field contains space-separated keyword values
. **Ref** field must be set to `REF**`
. **Value** field must match footprint filename (ignoring the `.kicad_mod` extension)
